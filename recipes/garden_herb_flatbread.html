Garden Herb Flatbread

Desription

"Even the smallest person can change the course of the future."

Ingredients

  • 7 g - active dry yeast
  • 1 tbsp - olive oil
  • 1 1/2 cup - all purpose flour
  • 1 tsp - salt
  • 3/4 cup - water 110F
  • 1 tbsp - fresh thyme chopped
  • 1 tbsp - fresh rosemary chopped

Steps

  1. Combine yeast and warm water and let it sit for 5-10 minutes. Add flour, olive oil, salt, thyme, and rosemary. Mix until combined.
  2. Knead for 3 minutes until smooth. Place the dough into a lightly oiled bowl and cover. Let it proof for 1 hour or until double in size.
  3. Pace the dough on a lightly floured surface and cut it into 6 equal size pieces. Roll each piece out with a rolling pin.
  4. At this point, you can grill or cook the dough on a skillet with a bit of oil until golden on both sides.
